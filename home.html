About the Camp

Our Cybersecurity Summer Camp is a unique experience for young enthusiasts looking to delve into the world of digital protection. Our camp provides a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills, covering topics such as ethical hacking, digital forensics, and network security.

With state-of-the-art facilities and expert instructors, campers will engage in real-world scenarios, ensuring they leave with a profound understanding of protecting digital assets.

Camp Schedule

Week 1: Introduction to Cybersecurity Principles

Week 2: Ethical Hacking and Systems Defense

Week 3: Cryptography and Secure Communication

Week 4: Cybersecurity Project and Capture The Flag Challenge
